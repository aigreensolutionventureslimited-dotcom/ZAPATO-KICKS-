Greenhouse Farming Plan for 50m x 100m Plot

Commercial Agriculture in Nigeria | Optimized Layout & Financial Projections

Land Assessment: 50m x 100m (5,000m²)

A standard plot of land in Nigeria (50m x 100m = 5,000m²) is more than sufficient for a profitable commercial greenhouse operation. In fact, this size is considered ideal for beginners and medium-scale farmers.

Key Advantages of This Plot Size

  • Enough space for 2-3 commercial greenhouses (1,000-1,500m² each)
  • Room for supporting infrastructure (nursery, storage, water system)
  • Buffer zones to prevent pest migration from neighboring farms
  • Future expansion capacity without acquiring additional land
  • Optimal for accessing government agricultural support programs

Recommended Crops for Beginners

🍅 Tomatoes

Cycle: 3 months

Yield: 3,000-4,000 kg per 500m²

Market Price: ₦300-₦500/kg

Monthly Revenue: ₦900,000-₦2M

🥒 Cucumbers

Cycle: 2.5 months

Yield: 2,500-3,500 kg per 500m²

Market Price: ₦400-₦600/kg

Monthly Revenue: ₦1M-₦2.1M

🫑 Sweet Peppers

Cycle: 4 months

Yield: 2,000-3,000 kg per 500m²

Market Price: ₦500-₦800/kg

Monthly Revenue: ₦1M-₦2.4M

Recommendation: Start with tomatoes or cucumbers as they have shorter cycles and consistent market demand in Nigeria.
